The Best Movies According to Durga Prasad

My top 4 movies of all-time.

Darling

This is a very feel good love story of all time.

Pushpa

This is movie is all about the attitude of the lead actor who is also the hero of the movie, Allu Arjun.

Bahubali

It is a movie which created a major impact on the Indian film industry. It is a PAN Indian film which is released across India and made the highest gross in the entire India. It also released in other countries also. It is directed by one of the most successful directors in our country, S. S. Rajamouli.

RRR

This is a PAN Indian film which released across India. It is about the friendship between two freedom fighters of India. This is also directed by the most successful director S. S. Rajamouli.
